Covid-19 Update: Broadway Bridges Tickets Available for Fall 2021...

With guidance from Governor Andrew Cuomo, based on New York State, New York City and CDC guidelines, as well as the state’s final approval of each theatre operator’s health and safety protocols, the Broadway League has announced that Broadway shows in New York City are now on sale for Fall 2021 performances.

Anticipated dates for each returning and new Broadway production will be announced on a show-by-show basis as individual productions and their producers determine the performance schedules and timelines for their respective shows. Broadway Bridges partners are encouraged to visit BroadwayBridges.org weekly for updated availability, show times and performances.

Our Mission:

To give every New York City public high school student the chance to see a Broadway Show.

Are you a teacher or administrator at a public high school in New York City?

1.

Register

Submit our request form. Once we've confirmed your eligibility, you will be notified when a new block of tickets go on sale.

2.

Request

Twice a year, Broadway Bridges participants are able to request tickets to a selection of shows. Please see guidelines for best practices before you apply.

3.

Attend

Once tickets are confirmed, we require attendance. We hope you and your students have a wonderful time!
