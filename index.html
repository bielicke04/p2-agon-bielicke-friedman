Our Mission:

To give every New York City public high school student the chance to see a Broadway Show.

Are you a teacher or administrator at a public high school in New York City?

1.

Register

Submit our request form. Once we've confirmed your eligibility, you will be notified when a new block of tickets go on sale.

2.

Request

Twice a year, Broadway Bridges participants are able to request tickets to a selection of shows. Please see guidelines for best practices before you apply.

3.

Attend

Once tickets are confirmed, we require attendance. We hope you and your students have a wonderful time!
